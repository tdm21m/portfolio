Overview

At Mara, the focus shifted from optimization to definition. The work centered on helping shape product primitives—what the system is, what it enables, and how users mentally model it from first interaction.

This required moving fluidly between product strategy, interaction design, and technical feasibility. The work emphasized rapid concept modeling, system framing, and prototype-driven decision making.
