Overview

Working inside the Microsoft collaboration ecosystem meant designing for scale measured in organizations, not users. The challenge was less about interface mechanics and more about shaping how real-world decision behavior translates into digital systems.

My work focused on reducing the distance between conversation and execution—designing experiences that help teams move from discussion to documented outcomes, accountability, and operational follow-through.

Operating inside a multi-surface platform environment required constant balancing between platform constraints, accessibility, performance, and interaction clarity. The goal was always the same: make complex collaboration feel inevitable and frictionless.
