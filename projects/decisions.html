Overview

Decisions uses AI to transform how organizations capture and act on meeting outcomes. No more lost action items, forgotten commitments, or unclear ownership.

I joined as the first designer to establish the product's design foundation, visual language, and core interaction patterns—from zero to launch.

Challenge

Meetings are where decisions happen, but the information generated is often lost. Notes are inconsistent, action items fall through the cracks, and institutional knowledge evaporates.

The challenge was to design an AI system that felt like a helpful team member rather than surveillance—building trust while extracting maximum value from every conversation.

Solution

I designed a meeting intelligence platform that automatically identifies decisions, action items, and key topics from conversations. The AI surfaces insights in real-time while remaining unobtrusive.

The interface emphasizes transparency—users can always see why the AI flagged something and correct it if needed. This builds trust and improves the model over time.
