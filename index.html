About

Designing systems,
not screens.

My path through design has been unconventional: from art direction to brand systems, from field research in Lagos to enterprise platforms at Microsoft.

Each transition deepened my understanding that great products aren't designed in isolation—they emerge from systems thinking, deep user empathy, and cross-functional collaboration.

Today, I focus on the intersection of AI and human collaboration: how do we design systems that augment human decision-making rather than replace it? How do we scale enterprise software without losing the craft?
