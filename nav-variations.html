Back Button — 5 Variations

The home page nav will replace "A Catalogue of Work" with the sun/moon toggle. These five variations explore how project pages handle the back button alongside that toggle — each with different tradeoffs in balance, clarity, and feel.

Back Moves to Right

Theme toggle stays left (matching home page). The back link replaces the mail icon on the right — displayed as a minimal arrow icon only. Structurally mirrors the home page perfectly.
